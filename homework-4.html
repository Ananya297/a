Novoneel Chakraborty is an Indian Author of fiction. He was born on 27 October[when?]. His specialty lies in romantic thrillers that he writes along with philosophy mixed with it. His first book "A Thing beyond forever"[1] released in 2008 and won the national bestseller tag in just a few months of its release. He followed it up with "That kiss in the rain"[2][3] and then wrote the book that won him the biggest applause, "How about a sin tonight?"[4] He went on to write commercial bestsellers like "Ex" and then "The Stranger Trilogy". The first two books of the trilogy- "Marry Me, Stranger" and "All Yours, Stranger" have won many hearts. He next released "Forget me not, Stranger"; the final installment of the trilogy. The stranger trilogy is stated to release in 6 different languages all over India by 2018. The series is also soon to be adapted to a 3-season webseries by Applause Entertainment in partnership with film-maker Goldie Behl.
